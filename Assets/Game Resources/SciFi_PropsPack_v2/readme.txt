Asset contains pack of props models (aerials, lockers, boxes, containers, terminals, solar panels) intended to use in sci-fi locations. The asset could be used in FPS genre games.</br>

This asset is optimized and hand tweaked.</br>

Triangles count: from 400 to 2500 tris (per model).</br>

The asset has 3 textures sets with a resolution from 2048*2048 to 4096*4096. </br>

Following textures included: base color, normal map, illumination, roughness and metalness maps.</br>

Pivots placed in logic places of geometry for most convenient to use.</br>
